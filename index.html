Instructions

  1. Input grid dimensions and click "Create Grid"
  2. Drag track pieces to place or move them on the grid
  3. Left-click or tap to rotate
  4. Right-click or long press on mobile to delete
  5. Use "Fill Empty Spaces" to add blank pieces to all empty squares
  6. Save your track and print to play at home
